Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI assistance work?
Our AI analyzes course content to generate transcripts, summaries, and personalized study recommendations. It also powers our smart Q&A system that can answer student questions in real-time.
Can I collaborate with other students?
Absolutely! Each course has dedicated discussion rooms, group projects spaces, and live chat features. You can study together, share notes, and even form study groups.
Are there certificates for completed courses?
Upon completing a course, you'll unlock advanced learning paths and gain recognition within the LearnX community. Our focus is on real understanding, practical skills, and measurable growth.
How much does LearnX cost?
We offer both free and premium courses. The free tier gives you access to basic content, while premium unlocks all features, certificates, and advanced content. Monthly and annual plans are available.
